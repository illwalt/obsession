About Obsession Hair Design

Obsession Hair Design is a family run Hairdresser's and Barbershop situated in Walthamstow, East London. Established in 1990 and currently celebrating our 30th year in business

We pride ourselves on providing a professional and courteous service within a warm, friendly and inviting environment.

We provide a variety of hair services for both Women and Men, catering to Afro, European and Mixed textured hair types.

Services we offer

  • Barbering Service
  • Weave
  • Extensions
  • Custom Wig Creation
  • Styling & Cutting
  • Hair Colouring:
  • Semi & Permanent hair colouring
  • Full head tint
  • Tint regrowth
  • Full & 1/2 head highlights
  • Hair Treatments:
  • Deep Conditioning
  • Moisturising & Protein
  • Dry & Itchy Scalp
  • Relaxing / Straightening
  • Perming
